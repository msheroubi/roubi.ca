Automation

Our role is to build tailored solutions that optimize your resource allocation, even for tasks that seem too complex to be automated. Many make the mistake of dismissing automation when tasks cannot be fully automated.

Even for tasks & processes that are meant to be manually executed (e.g. data annotation), the right automation streamlines the process until there are no wasted resources.

Our solutions have increased individual output by 6x-20x (depending on the task), and led to projects finishing up to 6 months ahead of schedule.

The process

We take on the role of a new hire. We shadow your team to understand the process, identifying any bottlenecks or redundancies. Depending on the complexity of the tasks and data, this process can take anywhere from 1-3 weeks and is free!

From there we build a solution tailored to your team's needs. We train them on how to use the it, and provide ongoing support as needed. This is an iterative process so you can expect to start seeing results right away.

Every step of the way is thoroughly documented so that you can take over the process at any time.
